# Radiant Revival

> baby, do you know what you wanna hear?

---

**Radiant Revival** is a work-in-progress sky rendering and lighting overhaul mod for Terraria 1.4.5.

## references

Previous implementations of the sky rendering have existed in:

- [ZenTheMod/ZensSky](https://github.com/ZenTheMod/ZensSky):
  - first iteration, later evolved into `sky-projects`;
- [gold-meridian/sky-projects](https://github.com/gold-meridian/sky-projects):
  - second iteration, eventually spiritually merged into Radiant Revival in lieu of 1.4.5's sky changes and a desire to also make lighting changes.

Inspiration for lighting changes have been sourced from the most popular tModLoader lighting mods:

- [Fancy Lighting](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2822950837):
  - our personal favorite in terms of pre-existing mods, the initial feature parity goal;
- [Lights And Shadows](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2670628346):
  - rather simple in how it works, but has issues because of it, mostly a feature parity goal;
- [Dynamic Lights](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2679594765):
  - main inspiration for well-defined directional lighting with tile geometry shadow casting.
